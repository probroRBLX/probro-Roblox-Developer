About me

Who I Am

I'm probro, a Roblox developer with 2.7 years of hands-on experience building games and gameplay systems. Throughout that time, I've created a wide range of features, including UI, inventories, shops, matchmaking, progression, data saving, animations, gamepasses, leaderboards, round systems, quests, and much more. My goal isn't just to make features work—it's to build systems that are clean, reliable, and easy to expand as a game grows. While there's always something new to learn, I've gained experience across most aspects of Roblox development and enjoy tackling both technical challenges and creative gameplay ideas.
